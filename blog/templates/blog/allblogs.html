Barrel Lift Design

In my Second year of Mechanical Engineering at Western University I was tasked with the design of a barrel lift. The lift had a list of requirements such as, 10,000 USD cost limit, Load capacity, as well as a variety of maneuverability requirements. The main purpose of this project however was to develop and prove my design, and CAD skills.
